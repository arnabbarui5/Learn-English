Present Tense


A tense expressing an action that is currently going on or habitually performed, or a state that currently or generally exists.

The present tense is mainly classified into four parts:

  • 1. Simple Present

  • 2. Present Perfect

  • 3. Present Continous

  • 4. Present Perfect Continous
